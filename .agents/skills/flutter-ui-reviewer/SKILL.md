Review the Flutter UI implementation.

## App Review Checklist

Check:
1. Does the app still feel like a tool, or does it feel like a polished learning product?
2. Is the main learning flow clear?
3. Are colors, spacing, radius, and text styles consistent?
4. Are buttons and cards visually unified?
5. Are pronunciation buttons clear for US and UK audio?
6. Are example sentence cards readable?
7. Is read-aloud scoring encouraging?
8. Are loading, empty, error, recording, scoring, and success states designed?
9. Is the share card feature beautiful and modular?
10. Are share card templates visually different?
11. Are pages safe on small Android devices?
12. Are repeated patterns extracted into reusable widgets?

## Required Output

Return:
- UI issues found
- Code-level improvement suggestions
- High-confidence fixes applied
- Remaining risks
